Rishi Ayyer (or The Sage, as his friends know him) is a Mechanical Engineer who currently works at National Instruments. Based out of Mumbai Bangalore, he enjoys traveling, eating, listening to (good != Trance) music, strumming a few chords on his guitar while singing, reading, writing short stories & poems and watching football.

Having completed his degree from IIT Jodhpur, he dreams of globe-trotting and writing his own little storybook of #OneMinuteFiction. He has done a two-year stint of Satire writing/editing and the works at News That Matters Not, India’s leading student satire website. He has also completed short internships at Motilal Oswal Asset Management Company and Tata Motors. He headed his College Festival’s Marketing Team while in College, and has experience in various other leadership positions. He publishes his blog on A Tear In The Rain, shares knowledge at Quora, and envisions a cleaner greener planet. (Just like you do.)
